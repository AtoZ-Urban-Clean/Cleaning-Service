Basic House Cleaning

General Areas: Dusting all accessible surfaces and furniture, removing cobwebs, vacuuming/mopping all floors, and emptying trash bins. Kitchen: Wiping down countertops, sink, faucet, and exterior of appliances. Bathroom: Scrubbing and disinfecting toilet, sink, shower/tub and mirrors. Bedrooms/Living Areas: Dusting furniture, making beds, and light tidying.
